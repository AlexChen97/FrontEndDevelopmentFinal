What the Heck is Investing?!

 Investing is the key to achievine FIRE. Basically, investing is putting your money to work. Your money will make more money for you while you sleep. When you have enough invested, it will generate enough income for you to live off of and when your investments generate more money than you need to live you will never run out of money! When you invest your money something called Compound Interest makes your money grow faster, its literally your money makeing more money. You will use that power to achive FIRE. Your probably asking, "How can I start investing?"



How do I Start?

  In order to invest by buying a stock you need what is called a Brokerage. A brokerage is a legal company that allows people to buy and sell investments. When you buy a stock it is a piece of a company you are buying. Think of any major company who's products you might own like Apple or Amazon. You can buy a piece of those companies and as they make money then the price of the stock goes up and you make money. You can check out a free brokerage like Robinhood to get started.

I have a brokerage, now what?

 Here is where the fun begins! You can officially start investing. To buy an investment you just select the one you want, input how much and how many you want then click Confirm. After that just consistantly invest in good companies. To find out more about investing and what you might want to invest into you can check out Investopedia



How do I Get Rich from Investing?

  Thats a great question! Tha answer was mentioned above... Compound Interest! Compound intrest is basically your money going to work to earn more money and that money that was earned will in turn make more money of its own. If you take a look at the chart on the right you can see that if you invested 200k over 30 years, your 200k would be worth 2 million! Thats a 10x return on your investment, and thats what will help you achieve FIRE.
