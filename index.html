What the Heck is F.I.R.E?

  F.I.R.E is an acronmy for Financial Independence, Retire Early. It is a movement of people who live frugally, save and invest to the point where they can quit their jobs and never give another day of their lives to a boss. Have you ever thought about never having to work to work for money again? Being able to live off the interest that your investments generate is the ultimate goal of F.I.R.E, total financial Independence, The freedom to do live life on your own terms.
